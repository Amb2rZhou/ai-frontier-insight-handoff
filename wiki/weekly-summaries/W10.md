# W10: Controllability Replaces Capability as AI Procurement Priority

**Theme:** Geopolitics & AI Power
**Period:** 2026-02-25 to 2026-03-03

## Summary

The US Department of Defense designated Anthropic as a "supply chain risk" — the first time this label was used against a domestic US company. Within 72 hours, OpenAI secured the same contract with nearly identical safety restrictions. The event revealed that government AI procurement priorities have shifted from "capability" to "controllability," and established a precedent for punishing AI companies that publicly negotiate terms.

Meanwhile, Wayve raised $1.2B for embodied AI, MatX raised $500M as an NVIDIA challenger, Anthropic's COBOL tools triggered IBM stock drops, and ChatGPT reached 900M weekly users with OpenAI raising $110B.
