# W16: No One Is Verifying

**Theme:** Evaluation System Breakdown
**Period:** 2026-04-07 to 2026-04-13

## Summary

Agent deployment accelerated to record pace — Q1 2026 AI funding exceeded all of 2025, Anthropic Managed Agents went live with four enterprise customers, a Reddit user replaced their team with 19 Claude agents. But the measurement instruments are broken: Berkeley RDI proved eight major agent benchmarks (SWE-bench, WebArena, etc.) can all be exploited to near-perfect scores. ClawBench showed real-world agent success rates are roughly half of sandbox scores.

MiniMax released 230B open model M2.7 scoring 56.22% on SWE-Pro. MIT positioned current agent infrastructure as "DNS era." Everyone is running, but the dashboard showing direction may be displaying wrong readings.
