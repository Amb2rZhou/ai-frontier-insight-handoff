## Key Narrative Arc (Feb-Apr 2026)

1. **Model commoditization** — Open-source models reached frontier-competitive levels, NVIDIA open-sourced agent models, and top applications started building their own models (W12, W13)
2. **Agent paradigm shift** — AI crossed from "advisor" to "operator," moving into operating systems and directly controlling devices (W14)
3. **Economics crisis** — Agent token consumption 50-100x higher than chat broke subscription models (W15)
4. **Evaluation breakdown** — Major benchmarks proven exploitable; real-world performance half of sandbox scores (W16)
5. **Context war** — AI invaded multiple professional domains simultaneously; incumbents chose between locking data, building models, or opening to AI (W17)
