## Weekly Insights
- **W13 (Water Sellers' Water Getting Cheaper)**: CEO highlighted that Cursor's Kimi-based model validated Chinese open-source impact on the global AI product stack, and proposed building HuggingFace into "S3 for Agents" — infrastructure for storing and serving agent data, positioning the platform as the neutral public layer beneath the proprietary agent wars.
