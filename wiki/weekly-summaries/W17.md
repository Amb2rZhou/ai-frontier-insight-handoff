# W17: The Context War

**Theme:** Strategic Choice
**Period:** 2026-04-14 to 2026-04-20

## Summary

AI simultaneously entered five+ professional domains in 48 hours: Anthropic launched Claude Design (challenging Figma), OpenAI upgraded Codex to computer operator and debuted GPT-Rosalind for life sciences, Salesforce transformed its CRM into agent infrastructure. Incumbents face three options for their most valuable asset — user context: lock it down, build their own models, or proactively open it as AI infrastructure.

Salesforce, Shopify, and SAP chose openness. But while incumbents debated, model companies were rapidly accumulating their own context through persistent memory, self-learning agents, and millions of user interactions. The window for this defensive war is closing.
